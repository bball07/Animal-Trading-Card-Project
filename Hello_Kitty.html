Hello Kitty





Hello Kitty is the popular cartoon character created in 1974 by the Sanrio Company of Japan.


    Full name: Kitty White
    Birthday: November 1, 1974
    Blood type: A
    Height: 3 Apples
    Weight: 5 Apples

Hello Kitty made her debut in 1974. It wasn't until 1976 that Hello Kitty was available in the United States. Hello Kitty is modeled after a Japanese bobtail cat. Her parents' names are George and Mary. Hello Kitty has a twin sister named Mimmy.
