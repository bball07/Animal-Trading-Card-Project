Mandarin Fish





The Mandarin fish has a bad smell to protect it from preditors.


    Scientific Name: Synchiropus splendidus
    Kingdom: Animalia
    Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
    Behavior: Peaceful and Shy

The Mandarin fish is a popular salt water fish that is hard to keep as a pet because of its unique diet of worms, fish eggs, gastropods, and copepods.It is also one of only two confirmed spiecies that produces its own unique blue colouring.The Mandarin fish is covered in tiny spikes that secrete a very toxic mucus. The Mandarin fish also does not have scales. You can find this fish in the Pacific Ocean, Japan, and Australia.
